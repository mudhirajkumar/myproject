DAL HOUSIE

Dalhousie is a hill station located in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is a popular tourist destination known for its natural beauty and scenic views of the Himalayas. Here is some basic information about Dalhousie: Location: Dalhousie is located in the district of Chamba in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India. It is situated at an altitude of 1,970 meters above sea level. Climate: The climate of Dalhousie is cool and pleasant throughout the year. Summers are mild with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 30°C, while winters are cold with temperatures dropping below freezing point. Attractions: Dalhousie is a popular tourist destination, and there are several places to visit and things to do. Some of the popular attractions include Khajjiar, Dainkund Peak, Panchpula, Satdhara Falls, and Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary. Accommodation: There are plenty of accommodation options in Dalhousie ranging from budget hotels to luxury resorts. Some of the popular hotels in Dalhousie are Hotel Mount View, Hotel Grand View, and Hotel Peace Channels. Transportation: The nearest airport to Dalhousie is the Gaggal Airport in Kangra, which is around 135 km away. The nearest railway station is Pathankot, which is around 80 km away. There are also regular bus services from Delhi, Chandigarh, and other nearby cities to Dalhousie. Activities: Some of the popular activities to do in Dalhousie include trekking, camping, paragliding, and sightseeing. The town also has several markets and shopping areas where you can buy local handicrafts and souvenirs.
