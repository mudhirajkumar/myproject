KASOL

Kasol is a hamlet in the Kullu district of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh.[3][4] It is situated in Parvati Valley, on the banks of the Parvati River, Between Bhuntar and Manikaran, it is located 30 km (19 mi) from Bhuntar and 36 km (22 mi) from Kullu town, the district headquarters, 3.5 km from Manikaran. Kasol is the Himalayan hotspot for backpackers[5] and acts as a base for nearby treks to Malana and Kheerganga. It is called Mini Israel of India due to a high percentage of Israeli tourists here.[6] Climate Kasol has good weather for several months of the year and receives a fair amount of snowfall from late December to February.
